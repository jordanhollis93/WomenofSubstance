About the seminar

Leslie Gray is a successful entrepreneur, author, speaker and motivational breast cancer survivor who has been offering support through sharing her cancer survival story with women’s groups and 501(c)(3) organizations for over 15 years. As a co-founder of GrowthPort Partners, Inc., recently featured in OPRAH and Entrepreneur Magazine, Leslie shares her first-hand perspective on women in business and the unique dynamics they face, where she has grown in wisdom and experience for over 20 years. Leslie is invited in to some of the country’s largest privately-held companies to provide her motivational insight, that when applied, engage, re-energize, transform and motivate women of all ages in every position, to foster more symbiotic relationships with one another. As unique as a fingerprint, Leslie’s principals for success through supportive collaboration result in lasting change both in life, relationships and the corporate world. No matter the topic, Leslie’s audiences enjoy the way she educates, illustrates, entertains, inspires and delivers!
